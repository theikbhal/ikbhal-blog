## Gopi's Story — PrepPilot, the WhatsApp Message, and a Founder's Itch

Gopi messaged me on July 21. The chat started simple:

> **Me:** Hi Gopi, how are you? What are you working on? Any progress on monetisation, marketing, sales?
>
> **Gopi:** Check Microsaas group. I tagged you in it.

Then he dropped this:

> **Gopi:** I am working on this problem. https://preppilot.study/
>
> I was scratching my own itch. Sort of felt it might be similar pain for most other parents and hence started productizing this.
>
> My elder one, almost always, says "I am done for the exam". The burden shifts towards me to expose his weak areas and force him into studying.
>
> So, I created a pre-exam setup using a web page that I built and exposed his weak area for that particular unit test. He felt grounded and continued his study.
>
> I was too lazy to do it manually and the same task every time. Started building a reusable framework + configurable questions. Then expanded the same for other parents to use.

That's the origin story. A father, a son who says "I'm done studying," and a lazy dad who automated the follow-up.

### What He Built

[PrepPilot](https://preppilot.study/) turns school practice answers into clear revision plans. A parent assigns a chapter check, the child answers, and the AI identifies weak areas with evidence — not guesses.

It's live for **ICSE Classes 7, 8, 9**. He has all chapters for Classes 6-10. **2 early adopters** already using it.
