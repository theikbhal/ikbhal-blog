## Nap at 2, Postponed to 3 — Mistake, Bath, 40 Mins Wasted

Planned a nap at 2 PM. Brain hit the 1:45 wall. Pushed it to 3.

Then I made a silly mistake — the kind that happens when you're running on empty. Got frustrated. Sat there scrolling, annoyed at myself, doing nothing useful. 40 minutes gone.

### The Bath That Should Have Come First

Finally took a bath. 10 minutes. Came out clear-headed, frustration gone.

That bath should have happened *before* the mistake. Before the 40 minutes of wasted nothing.

### New Rule

- Frustrated? Stop. Take a bath.
- Don't sit in the frustration. Don't push through the fog.
- 10 minutes of water saves 40 minutes of regret.

---

*Little win logged 🎉*
