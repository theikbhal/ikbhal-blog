## Nap at 1:45 — The ADHD Midday Reset

It's 1:45 PM. Nap time. Non-negotiable.

My brain hits a wall around this time every day. Focus dissolves. Eyes get heavy. Starting anything new feels impossible.

The fix: **15-20 minute nap**. No more, no less. Any longer and I wake up groggy for an hour.

I set an alarm, lie down, and just stop. No phone, no thoughts, no guilt about the 10 reels I haven't finished yet.

After the nap: back to work. Fresh enough to push through the afternoon.

This is part of the routine now. School runs, reels, coding, nap, then more reels. The day has rhythm.
